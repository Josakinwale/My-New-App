Introduction to Building Drawing

.

Building Drawing also known Architectural Drawing is the graphical representation of a proposed building showing its Elevations Plan and Sections of major parts (i.e. elements) such as Floors, Walls, Staircases, Foundations, Roof e.t.c.

Building Drawing shows the construction details of the building elements and helps the builder to construct the building to specifications.
