Floor Plan

A pictorial image of the floor plan of a building is shown on the left side.


A plan represents the top view of an object as seen on an horizontal plane. However, with buildings, floor plans are visualized as the top views of each floor as seen on the horizontal plane that cuts through all walls, doors, windows e.t.c. at about one metre above floor level. 

Floor plans are usually drawn to scale 1:100. If the roof of the building above is removed the result is as below.

Since the floor plan is the top view at about 1 metre above the floor, it can be presented as shown on the next page.
